SHIPPING & DELIVERY POLICY
- Standard delivery takes 3-5 business days.
- Express delivery takes 1-2 business days for an extra charge.
- If your order is delayed by more than 5 days, you are eligible for a Rs 50 coupon automatically applied to your account.
- Orders above Rs 500 get free standard shipping.
- You can track your order using the tracking ID sent to your email.

RETURN & REFUND POLICY
- Returns are accepted within 7 days of delivery.
- The product must be unused and in original packaging.
- Refunds below Rs 5000 are processed within 3-5 business days.
- Refunds above Rs 5000 require manager approval and take 5-7 business days.
- Damaged products are eligible for immediate replacement or full refund.
- To initiate a return, contact support with your order ID.

CANCELLATION POLICY
- Orders can be cancelled before they are shipped.
- Once shipped, cancellation is not possible — you must wait for delivery and then request a return.
- Cancelled orders are refunded within 2-3 business days.

DAMAGED / DEFECTIVE PRODUCT POLICY
- If you receive a damaged product, report it within 48 hours of delivery with photos.
- You are eligible for a free replacement or full refund.
- ShopEase covers return shipping for damaged products.

PAYMENT & OFFERS
- We accept UPI, credit card, debit card, and net banking.
- EMI is available on orders above Rs 3000.
- Coupons can be applied at checkout and cannot be combined with other offers.

ACCOUNT & MEMBERSHIP
- Regular members get standard support.
- VIP members (lifetime purchase above Rs 50000) get priority support and faster refunds.
- VIP members can reach our senior support team directly.
